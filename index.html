What is Chess?

Chess is a two-player board game played on an 8x8 grid. Each player starts with 16 pieces and the objective is to checkmate the opponent’s king. It is a game of deep strategy, critical thinking, and tactical play.

Phases of the Game

Opening

Develop pieces and control the center of the board.

Middlegame

Use tactics and strategy to attack, defend, and control space.

Endgame

With few pieces left, players aim to deliver checkmate or force a draw.
